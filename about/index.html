About

The Imaginary Gallery was dreamt up somewhere between Glasgow and London.

We want to challenge the way we consume art, both in galleries and across social media.

We want to see art shared in an inclusive way, where every visitor feels they can take up space and have a dialogue with the work. We want people to feel welcomed, not manipulated by algorithms, and able to think outside of social barriers in the physical world.

We believe in the power of art to foster empathy and bring us joy, all in a safe space away from the hectic everyday.

We hope you enjoy your visit to the gallery. We are organising an open call and can't wait to have an exciting range of works to show you. For more information about the open call, please click here. For now, please feel free to explore the gallery - the space is yours!
